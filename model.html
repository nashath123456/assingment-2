JIBSY

Maruti Suzuki has pulled off the production of its famous SUV car - Gypsy in India. The SUV is famous for its looks, strong engine and 4x4 drive. The 33-year-old Gypsy was Maruti's third car which entered the market after Maruti 800 and Omni. Gypsy production started in late 1985 and the company has now released an official notification about the discontinuation of the model
